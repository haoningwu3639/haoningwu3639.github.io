I am currently a 1st-year master student of MediaBrain at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Weidi Xie and Prof. Xiaoyun Zhang. I received my Bachelor degree in EE (IEEE honor class) from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2022, and continue my journey in SJTU.

I'm generally interested in computer vision, video processing and generative models. My current research interest lies in video synthesis and diffusion models. I'm curious and passionate about Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) and AI Generated Content (AIGC). I hope I can make some contribution to this community. Feel free to contact me via my email!
